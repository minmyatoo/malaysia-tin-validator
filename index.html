Malaysia TIN Validator

TIN Format Guide:
  • Individual (New): IG + 10 digits
  • Individual (Old): SG/OG + 9 digits
  • Non-Individual (New): C/D/E/F + 10 digits
  • Non-Individual (Old): C/D/E/F + 9 digits
  • General TINs: EI + 10 digits
